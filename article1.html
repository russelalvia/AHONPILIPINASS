Types of COVID-19 Test



During the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID19) pandemic, rapid testing, tracking, tracing, and isolating (TTTI) has proven critical to public health policy responses. COVID-19 tests of various types have been developed, each with advantages and disadvantages. Given their respective advantages and disadvantages, effective testing strategies can combine different tests to achieve complementarity.

Types of COVID-19 test

There are two types of COVID-19 tests designed to detect an active infection: molecular tests and rapid antigen tests. The antibody test, a third type of test, can be used at any time. The RT-PCR and LFT are antigen tests that are discussed below (1 and 2), followed by a brief look at the antibody test (3), which is designed to detect a previous encounter with the virus.

• RT-PCR
• Point of care RT-LAMP test
• CRISPR-based test
• Rapid antigen test
• Antibody test
